time_aliases¶

Astronomy.time_aliases(year, UTdoy, obs_long)¶

Time as days since 1900, centuries since 1900 and LST

Notes

This simplifies the output from ManGord.atime().

:param year : int

:param UTdoyfloat

Day of year and niversal time

:param obs_longfloat

Observatory west longitude in degrees

Returns

tuple of floats (number of days since 1900.5, the same in Julian centuries, and the local sidereal time in decimal days
